About DevHub Network

We are a global community of developers collaborating on open-source projects that make a difference.

Our Mission

DevHub Network connects developers worldwide to collaborate on meaningful open-source projects. We believe in the power of community-driven development to solve real-world problems.

Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small GitHub organization to a global network with thousands of contributors across 80+ countries.

Our approach combines mentorship for new developers, collaborative coding sessions, and hackathons to foster innovation and skill development.
